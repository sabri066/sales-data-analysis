# 📊 Sales Data Analysis — Product & Regional Performance

## 📌 Business Question
Which products and regions drive the most revenue — 
and where should the business focus its efforts?

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Tableau (Dashboard)

## 📂 Dataset
Practice dataset containing 11 sales transactions 
across 4 products and 4 regions.

## 🔄 Process
- Built and structured sales dataset using Pandas
- Calculated total sales by product and region
- Created visualizations using Matplotlib
- Built interactive Tableau dashboard

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Phone generated the highest total sales** (~21K) 
  making it the most valuable product in the portfolio. 
  The business should prioritize Phone inventory and promotions.

- **East region dominated all other regions** (~21.5K in sales) — 
  nearly 3x more than the North region (~7.5K). 
  This suggests East is the strongest market and deserves 
  focused investment.

- **Smart Watch had the lowest sales** (~3K total) despite 
  being in the product lineup — indicating either weak demand 
  or poor visibility that needs addressing.

- **Average transaction value was $4,499** — 
  driven by high-price items like Laptops ($1,200–$1,350).

## 📈 Tableau Dashboard
Includes two views:
- Sales by Product (bar chart)
- Sales by Region (bar chart)

## 🚀 Business Recommendation
Focus marketing budget on Phone products in the East region 
for maximum revenue impact. Investigate Smart Watch 
underperformance before continuing investment.
